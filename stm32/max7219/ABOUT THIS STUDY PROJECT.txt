In this project a LED strip WS2812 2m / 120 leds, display ili9341 320*240 with touch xpt2046, 4 matrix max7219 8*8 leds,
atmospheric sensors BMP180 and Si7021 and a variable resistor are connected to the board STM32F407ZET6 
The log is output through the module UART-USB to the computer.
